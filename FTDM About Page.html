"My Goal Is To Talk About
Faith And God
And Have Fun With It"

My name is David Rose, I am married to my wife Michelle and we have a son, Elliot. I am currently the pastor of two churches and I am passionate about reaching out to those who aren't comfortable in church, but don'tmind watching a short funny video. A church setting may not be for everyone, but I believe that the gospel is and I love to come up with new and creative ways to share it.

This show was born out of the idea of making the gospel accessable to anyone. "Church talk" or "faith talk" is awkward by default in our culture. My desire is to break down the usual barriers of entry and present the message in a clear, conscise and funny format.

My hope is to reach a wider audience than the typical "churchgoer" and perhaps even convince a few people that are currently anxious about church to give it a try!
